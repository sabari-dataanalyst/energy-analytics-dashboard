# 🌍 Energy Consumption / Usage Analysis Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing global energy consumption patterns using an interactive Power BI dashboard. It provides a comprehensive view of energy usage across countries, years, and energy sources.

## 🚀 Key Features
- 📈 Year-wise analysis of global energy consumption (TWh)
- 🌍 Geographic distribution of energy usage across regions
- ⚡ Country-wise comparison of energy consumption (KWh)
- 🔋 Energy source breakdown (Coal, Natural Gas, Hydroelectric)
- 💰 Energy price index analysis (USD/kWh)
- 📊 Key KPIs:
  - Total Energy Consumption
  - Energy Usage
  - Average Load Factor
  - Average Energy Price

## 📌 Insights Derived
- Energy consumption shows fluctuating growth trends over the years
- Certain countries dominate global energy usage
- Fossil fuels (Coal & Natural Gas) contribute a major share of energy production
- Renewable sources are present but comparatively lower
- Energy prices and load factors vary across regions and time

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Data Visualization)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- DAX (for calculated metrics)
